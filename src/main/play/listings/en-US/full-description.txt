This application adds Quebec City RTC buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.rtcquebec.ca and @RTCQuebec on Twitter.

RTC serves Quebec City in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de transport de la Capitale.
https://www.rtcquebec.ca/en/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-quebec-rtc-bus-android

This app is not related with Réseau de transport de la Capitale.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.rtcquebec.ca and Twitter.
